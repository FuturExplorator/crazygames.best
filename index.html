How to Play Monster Survivors

Basic Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Mouse to aim and shoot. Space for special abilities, and E to interact with objects.

Game Objective

Survive waves of monsters while collecting resources and power-ups. The longer you survive, the more points you earn!

Pro Tips

  • Always keep moving to avoid monster attacks
  • Collect power-ups whenever possible
  • Use special abilities strategically
  • Watch out for boss monsters
